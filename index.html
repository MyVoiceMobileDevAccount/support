Quick Help

  • Check that microphone permissions are enabled if speech input is not working.
  • Use the password reset flow if you cannot access your account.
  • Make sure your internet connection is active for login and cloud sync features.
  • Restart the app after changing device permissions.
  • Include screenshots when reporting layout or content issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the password reset option in the app. If you do not receive the reset email, check your spam or junk folder and then contact support.

Why is speech recognition not working?

Confirm that microphone and speech recognition permissions are enabled in iOS Settings. A stable connection may also be required depending on your setup.

My content is missing or not syncing.

Sign out and back in, then confirm you are using the correct account. If the issue continues, contact support with the email address tied to the account.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Send a short description of the problem or feature request to the support email. Clear reproduction steps help speed up review.

About MyVoice

MyVoice is an AAC-focused communication app built to help users express themselves through customizable words, groups, and speech tools in a simple interface.

This support page exists so App Store reviewers and end users have a direct, public place to get help with the app.
